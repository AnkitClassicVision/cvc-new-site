Our Optical

Contact
Lenses

Contact lenses at Classic Vision Care offer you the freedom to see clearly without glasses, whether you are playing sports, attending special events, or simply prefer a frames-free look. Our family-owned Kennesaw and Marietta practices have fitted thousands of patients over 25+ years of service, earning a 4.7-star rating from 756+ reviews. We carry daily disposables, bi-weekly and monthly lenses, toric lenses for astigmatism, multifocal lenses for presbyopia, and colored contacts for cosmetic enhancement. Our optometrists use advanced corneal mapping technology to measure your eye's unique curvature, ensuring each lens sits comfortably and provides crisp vision throughout your day. First-time contact lens wearers receive hands-on training for insertion, removal, and proper care. We also stock specialty brands for patients with dry eyes or sensitivity, so you never have to settle for lenses that cause discomfort. Schedule your contact lens fitting at either East Cobb or Kennesaw location and discover why local families trust us for their vision needs.

Lens Types

Contact Lens Options

Our Kennesaw and Marietta optometrists fit every major lens type, from budget-friendly monthly options to premium daily disposables. We match your prescription, tear film quality, and wearing schedule to the lens that will deliver the clearest, most comfortable vision for your lifestyle.

Daily Disposables

Fresh lens every day, a healthy and convenient option. No cleaning required.

Best for: Occasional wearers, allergies, dry eyes

Bi-Weekly & Monthly

Reusable lenses that offer great value. Require nightly cleaning and proper care.

Best for: Full-time wearers seeking value

Toric (for Astigmatism)

Specially designed lenses that correct astigmatism for clear, stable vision.

Best for: Astigmatism correction

Multifocal

See clearly at all distances (near, intermediate, and far) without reading glasses.

Best for: Presbyopia (age 40+)

Colored Contacts

Enhance or change your eye color with prescription or non-prescription options.

Best for: Cosmetic enhancement

Specialty Contacts

Scleral lenses, Ortho-K, and solutions for hard-to-fit eyes.

Contact Lens FAQs

A few quick answers before your fitting.

Do I need a contact lens exam if I already have a glasses prescription?

Yes. Contacts sit on the eye, so we need additional measurements and a fit evaluation to keep your eyes healthy and comfortable.

Are daily lenses better for dry eyes?

They can be a great option because you start with a fresh lens each day. We’ll recommend what fits your tear film and wearing schedule.

Can I wear contacts with astigmatism?

Often, yes. Toric lenses are designed to correct astigmatism and provide stable, clear vision when properly fit.

What if contacts have been uncomfortable for me before?

You may still have options. We can adjust lens material, replacement schedule, or explore specialty designs based on your needs.

Details That Matter

A more helpful guide to Contact Lenses

Great eyewear should feel effortless. The right frame fit, lens design, and coatings can change how you see and how you feel at the end of the day. Our optical team helps you choose options that match your work, hobbies, and comfort.

If you have struggled with glare, headaches, or eyestrain, your lenses may be part of the solution. We can recommend materials and designs that reduce reflections, improve night driving, and support long hours on screens.

We also make it easy to keep your glasses feeling right. If something feels loose or uncomfortable, bring them in for adjustments. We offer free glasses repairs, even if the frames were not purchased here.

Quick ways to get more value from your visit

  • Bring your current glasses so we can note what you love and what you want to change.
  • Tell us about your lifestyle, work setup, and hobbies so lens options match your needs.
  • Ask about lens coatings, blue-light strategies, and lightweight materials for comfort.
  • If your glasses feel loose or uncomfortable, bring them in. We offer free adjustments and repairs.

Common questions

Clear answers help you move forward with confidence.

Can you help me choose frames that fit my face?

Yes. Our opticians can guide fit, comfort, and style so you feel confident wearing your glasses every day. Small adjustments make a big difference.

What lens options should I consider?

Options depend on your prescription and lifestyle. We can discuss lightweight materials, anti-reflective coatings, computer lenses, and progressives if needed.

Do you offer repairs and adjustments?

Yes. We offer free glasses repairs and adjustments, even if you did not purchase your frames with us. Stop in and we will take a look.

How long does it take to get new glasses?

Timing depends on lenses and customization. We will set expectations when you order and keep you updated if anything changes.

Do you take vision insurance for glasses?

Many plans include vision benefits, but coverage varies. Bring your insurance information and we will help you understand copays, allowances, and any out-of-pocket cost before you place an order.

Can you help with progressives?

Yes. Progressive lenses are highly customizable. We help you choose a design that fits your prescription and habits, then take careful measurements so the lenses feel natural in daily use.
