Comprehensive Eye Care

Comprehensive Eye Care Services

Looking for eye care services in Kennesaw? Classic Vision Care is here to help with clear answers and an easy next step.

  • Clear next steps and a simple booking path
  • Friendly support from our team
  • Care built around your goals

Core Services

Eye Examinations

Comprehensive vision and eye health assessments tailored to your unique needs, using advanced diagnostic equipment.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Our thorough eye examinations go beyond just checking your vision. We use advanced diagnostic technology including OCT imaging, digital retinal photography, and automated visual field testing to evaluate your complete eye health.

  • Complete vision assessment and prescription update
  • Glaucoma, cataract, and macular degeneration screening
  • Detection of systemic health conditions
Learn more about our exams

Pediatric Eye Exams

Children's vision is critical for learning and development. Our pediatric exams are designed to be fun and comfortable while thoroughly assessing your child's visual system, including eye teaming, tracking, and focusing abilities.

  • Age-appropriate testing for infants through teens
  • Early detection of amblyopia (lazy eye) and strabismus
  • Learning-related vision assessment
Schedule your child's exam

Contact Lens Exams

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive contact lens evaluation. We offer the latest lens technologies including daily disposables, multifocal lenses, and lenses designed for digital device users and dry eye sufferers.

  • Precise corneal measurements and fitting
  • Trial lenses to ensure comfort and clarity
  • Training on insertion, removal, and care
Explore contact lens options

Diabetic Eye Exams

Diabetes can cause serious eye complications, often without early symptoms. Our specialized diabetic eye exams use advanced imaging to detect diabetic retinopathy and other diabetes-related eye conditions in their earliest stages.

  • Dilated retinal examination
  • OCT imaging for macular edema detection
  • Coordination with your primary care physician
Learn about diabetic eye care

Specialty Services

Our specialized treatments address complex vision needs with personalized care plans and modern technology.

Dry Eye Spa

Experience relief from chronic dry eye with our spa-like treatment center. We offer advanced therapies including IPL (Intense Pulsed Light), MiBoFlo thermal therapy, and BlephEx lid treatments.

  • OptiLight IPL therapy
  • MiBoFlo thermal treatment
  • BlephEx lid cleaning
Visit Dry Eye Spa
Featured

Myopia Control

Protect your child's future vision with our myopia management program. We use evidence-based treatments to slow the progression of nearsightedness and reduce the risk of serious eye conditions later in life.

  • Orthokeratology (Ortho-K)
  • MiSight contact lenses
  • Atropine therapy
Learn About Myopia Control

Specialty Contact Lenses

For patients with challenging prescriptions or corneal conditions, we offer expert fitting of specialty lenses that can provide clearer, more comfortable vision when standard lenses fall short.

  • Scleral lenses for keratoconus
  • Rigid gas permeable lenses
  • Post-surgical lens fitting
Explore specialty options

Details That Matter

A more helpful guide to Comprehensive Eye Care Services

We believe you deserve eye care that feels clear, calm, and personal. Our team takes time to explain findings and help you choose the next step that fits your life.

If you are not sure which appointment type to schedule, call us. We will help you choose the right visit so you get answers efficiently.

Quick ways to get more value from your visit

  • Share any vision changes, headaches, dryness, or glare issues you have noticed.
  • Bring a list of medications and eye drops so we can factor them into your care plan.
  • Tell us about your work and screen time so your prescription supports your day-to-day life.
  • If you drive at night, mention halos or glare. Small lens adjustments can make a big difference.

Common questions

Clear answers help you move forward with confidence.

How do I get started?

Pick the location that works best for you and book an appointment. If you are unsure which service to choose, call us and we will help you decide.

Do you have two locations?

Yes. We serve patients in Kennesaw and East Cobb/Marietta. You can choose the office that fits your schedule.

What should I bring?

Bring your current glasses or contacts, photo ID, and insurance card if applicable. Let us know about any symptoms so we can plan the visit.

How quickly can I be seen?

We offer a range of appointment times. For urgent concerns, call and we will do our best to help you as soon as possible.
