The Dry Eye Spa Experience

Spa-Like Relief for Dry Eyes

Tired of eye drops every hour? At our Dry Eye Spa in Atlanta, we treat the root cause of your discomfort, not just the symptoms. Our spa-inspired approach combines clinical expertise with therapeutic comfort. Many patients tell us the MiBoFlo treatment is so relaxing they nearly fall asleep.

What Makes It a Dry Eye Spa

Most dry eye treatment is clinical and uncomfortable. We've created something different. Our spa-like environment combines clinical results with genuine comfort, so you can relax while we restore your eyes.

Comfort-First Care

Warm compresses, gentle therapies, and a calming environment.

Personalized Plans

Treatment recommendations tailored to your symptoms and lifestyle.

Advanced Technology

MiBoFlo, IPL, BlephEx, punctal plugs, and scleral lenses.

Ongoing Relief

Maintenance options and home care guidance for lasting results.

Common Dry Eye Symptoms

If any of these sound familiar, our Dry Eye Spa can help.

  • Burning or stinging
  • Gritty or sandy feeling
  • Redness and irritation
  • Watery eyes
  • Blurred vision
  • Contact lens discomfort
