Details That Matter

A more helpful guide to Your Trusted Eye Doctor in East Cobb & Marietta

Choosing an eye doctor is about trust and convenience. Classic Vision Care serves families across Kennesaw and East Cobb/Marietta, and we make it easy to pick the location that fits your schedule.

Our visits are designed to feel calm, thorough, and clear. We use modern diagnostic tools, and we take time to explain results so you know exactly what is happening and what comes next.

If you need eyewear support, you can also browse frames, ask about lens options, and get adjustments. If you have glasses that need quick attention, ask about our free repairs.

If you are unsure what type of appointment to schedule, call the office. We will help you choose the right visit so you get answers efficiently.

Quick ways to get more value from your visit

  • Arrive 10 to 15 minutes early if you are new, so we can get you checked in comfortably.
  • Bring your current glasses and contact lenses, plus any recent prescriptions if you have them.
  • Plan for dilation if recommended, and consider bringing sunglasses for the drive home.
  • Ask about free glasses repairs. We are happy to help even if the frames were not purchased here.

Common questions

Clear answers help you move forward with confidence.

Which location should I choose?

Both offices provide comprehensive eye care. If you have a specific service in mind or want the soonest appointment, call and we will recommend the best location for you.

Where should I park?

Parking is available near the office. If you have mobility needs or need help finding the entrance, call ahead and we will guide you.

Do you take walk-ins?

Scheduling is the best way to guarantee time with the doctor, especially for contact lens fittings and medical evaluations. If you need a same-day visit, call and we will check openings.

What if I need to update my glasses?

You can browse frames, ask for lens recommendations, and get adjustments. If your current glasses need a quick repair, bring them in and we will take a look.

Do you offer evening or weekend appointments?

Hours vary by location and day. The easiest way to find a time is to book online or call, and we will help you choose an opening that fits your schedule.

Can I transfer my care between locations?

Yes. We can coordinate your records so you can visit the office that is most convenient, including for follow-ups and eyewear adjustments.

Do you see urgent eye problems?

If you have an eye concern that feels urgent, call the office. We will help you understand the next step and schedule you as soon as possible when appropriate.

Is the office family-friendly?

Yes. Many of our patients are families, and we work to make visits comfortable for kids, teens, and adults.

Do you have two locations?

Yes. We serve patients in Kennesaw and East Cobb/Marietta. You can choose the location that best fits your schedule.

Can I schedule family appointments back-to-back?

Often, yes. Call the office and we will help coordinate times that keep your day simple.
