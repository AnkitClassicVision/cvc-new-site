Eye Emergency?

If you experience a sudden vision change, eye injury, severe eye pain, flashes of light, floaters, or a foreign object in your eye, contact Classic Vision Care immediately during office hours. Our Kennesaw and East Cobb optometrists reserve time slots for urgent eye care needs and can often see you the same day. Prompt treatment of eye emergencies can prevent permanent vision loss.
