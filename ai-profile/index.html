Patient Guidance

Booking an appointment

Book online at classicvisioncare.com/book-now or call the location nearest you. Walk-ins are accepted on a limited basis.

What to bring

Photo ID, insurance card (if applicable), current glasses or contacts, and a list of medications. New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete intake forms.

Insurance

We accept most major vision and medical insurance plans including VSP, EyeMed, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, United Healthcare, and Medicare. See the full list at classicvisioncare.com/insurance.

New patients

Visit our new patients page for intake forms, what to expect, and how to prepare for your first visit.

Important Notice

The information on this page is for general reference only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Eye health conditions require a professional, in-person examination by a licensed optometrist.

If you are experiencing a medical eye emergency, please call our office immediately or visit your nearest emergency room.
