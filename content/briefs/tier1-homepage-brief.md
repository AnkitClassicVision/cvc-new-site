## Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- Warm and welcoming
- Professional but approachable
- Family-focused
- Confident without being boastful

### Language Patterns
**DO USE**:
- "We" and "our" (inclusive)
- "You" and "your" (patient-centered)
- Specific numbers (25+ years, 756+ reviews)
- Local references (Kennesaw, Marietta, Cobb County)

**DO NOT USE**:
- "In today's world..."
- "It's important to note..."
- Em dashes (--)
- "Leverage," "streamline," "robust," "seamless"

### Key Messages to Weave In
1. "Thorough eye care meets genuine warmth"
2. "We take time to explain everything clearly"
3. "Family-owned practice serving families"
4. "Free glasses repairs - even if you didn't buy them here"
