## Research Summary

### What is BlephEx?
- First and only professional eyelid cleaning procedure
- Patented handpiece with medical-grade micro-sponge
- Removes bacterial biofilm from eyelid margins
- Like dental cleaning for your eyelids
- Addresses blepharitis and dry eye blepharitis syndrome (DEBS)

### Conditions Treated
- Blepharitis (eyelid inflammation)
- Dry eye disease
- Dry Eye Blepharitis Syndrome (DEBS)
- Recurrent styes/chalazia
- Pre-surgical eyelid hygiene

### How It Works
- Spinning micro-sponge along eyelid edge
- Removes scurf, debris, bacterial biofilm
- Exfoliates eyelid margins
- Fresh sponge for each eyelid (4 total)
- Disposable, sterile components

### The Procedure
- Less than 10 minutes total
- Numbing drop for comfort
- Walk-in, walk-out procedure
- Most feel only tickling sensation
- No downtime

### Treatment Frequency
- Every 4-6 months typically
- Individual based on symptoms
- Home care between treatments
- Not a cure, but management tool

### Who is a Candidate?
- Chronic blepharitis patients
- Those unresponsive to home treatments
- Recurrent styes
- Pre-cataract surgery patients
- Anyone with lid margin debris
