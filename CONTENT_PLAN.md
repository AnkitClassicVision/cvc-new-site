## Adapted 17-Phase Process for Website Content

### Phase 1: Research & Strategy (Per Page)
- [ ] Keyword research (primary + 3-5 secondary)
- [ ] Competitor analysis (top 3 ranking pages)
- [ ] User intent mapping
- [ ] Content gap identification

### Phase 2: Outline & Structure
- [ ] H1 headline (keyword-optimized)
- [ ] H2 section structure (6-10 sections)
- [ ] Key points per section
- [ ] CTA placement strategy

### Phase 3: Content Creation
- [ ] Write engaging intro (problem/solution hook)
- [ ] Develop each section with:
  - Patient-focused language
  - Local references (Kennesaw, Marietta, East Cobb)
  - Trust signals (25+ years, 756+ reviews)
  - Specific benefits
- [ ] Add internal links (3-5 per page)
- [ ] Include relevant CTAs

### Phase 4: Voice & Quality
- [ ] Match brand voice (warm, professional, family-focused)
- [ ] Eliminate AI patterns (no em dashes, banned phrases)
- [ ] Ensure readability (Flesch-Kincaid grade 8-10)
- [ ] Fact-check all claims

### Phase 5: Technical SEO
- [ ] Meta title (under 60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 matches/supports meta title
- [ ] Image alt text optimization
- [ ] Schema markup (LocalBusiness, MedicalBusiness, FAQPage)

## Content Voice Guidelines

### Brand Personality
- **Warm & Welcoming:** "You're not a number here, you're family"
- **Professional but Approachable:** Expert knowledge without jargon
- **Local & Personal:** References to Kennesaw, Marietta, Cobb County
- **Trustworthy:** 25+ years experience, 756+ reviews

### Key Messages to Weave In
1. "Thorough eye care meets genuine warmth"
2. "We take time to explain everything clearly"
3. "Family-owned practice serving families"
4. "Free glasses repairs - even if you didn't buy them here"
5. "25+ years caring for the community"

### Banned Phrases (AI Patterns)
- "In today's world..."
- "It's important to note..."
- "Let's dive in..."
- "Leverage," "streamline," "robust," "seamless"
- "Furthermore," "Moreover," "Additionally" at paragraph starts

## Research Requirements Per Page Type

### Service Pages
- What is the service/procedure?
- Who needs it? (symptoms, conditions, demographics)
- What to expect during the visit
- Benefits over alternatives
- Recovery/aftercare (if applicable)
- Why choose CVC for this service?
- FAQs (3-5)

### Location Pages
- Specific address and directions
- Hours of operation
- Services offered at this location
- Meet the doctors at this location
- Patient testimonials (location-specific)
- Local landmarks/neighborhoods served
- Insurance accepted

### Condition Pages
- What is the condition?
- Symptoms and warning signs
- Risk factors
- How CVC diagnoses and treats it
- Prevention tips
- When to see a doctor
- FAQs

### Product Pages (Eyewear)
- Product types/options available
- Benefits and features
- Brands carried
- Fitting process
- Care and maintenance
- Insurance/payment options
