Combining Supplements & In-Office Treatments with Classic Vision

Explore the benefits of combining supplements with in-office treatments for improved eye health at Classic Vision Care.

July 29, 2025 • By Ankit Patel

Book an appointment Dry Eye Spa Locations

The Complete Vision Care Strategy: Supplements Plus Professional Treatment

Combining eye health supplements with in-office treatments is the best way to maintain your visual well-being. At Classic Vision Care, we treat conditions like dry eye, macular degeneration and age-related visual problems, so we understand the benefits of a holistic approach to eye care.

Why a Holistic Approach to Eye Health Matters

The role of supplements in eye health is well known, but it is still incredibly important to combine them with professional treatments. Vitamins, antioxidants and Omega-3s are used alongside in-office procedures such as dry eye treatments and vision therapy to create holistic eye care solutions that treat the problem from multiple angles.

Common Eye Conditions Treated with Supplements and In-Office Care

Some of the most common eye conditions that can benefit from both supplements and professional treatments include: 

  • Dry eye syndrome – dry eyes can be treated in a variety of ways, including using LipiFlow, punctal plugs and eye drops. Dry eye supplements and treatments, including Omega-3 and vitamin A, work alongside these procedures to provide lasting relief. 
  • Macular degeneration – macular degeneration, particularly age-related, may benefit from a mix of supplements including vitamins C and E, lutein, copper, zinc, and zeaxanthin to help slow the disease.
  • Diabetic retinopathy – diabetic eye care is extremely important so that conditions like diabetic retinopathy, cataracts, and diabetic macular edema are caught early. In-office therapies and the use of supplements are important in the management of these conditions.

The Science Behind Supplements for Eye Health

So, which of the many available eye health supplements can benefit your vision? 

The following are some of the vitamins, minerals and antioxidants that can support vision health: 

  • Omega-3 fatty acids: This supplement can reduce inflammation as well as help cornea and retina cells heal and regenerate following light exposure and aging. 
  • Lutein & zeaxanthin: These nutrients are known as carotenoids and may have a protective effect by absorbing ultraviolet and high-energy blue light, which can damage the eyes.
  • Vitamin C & E: Both of these vitamins act as antioxidants, working to protect the eyes from free radicals and reducing the risk of cataracts and age-related macular degeneration.

In-Office Treatments Available at Classic Vision Care

At Classic Vision Care, we offer the following advanced treatments: 

  • LipiFlow treatment: LipiFlow uses gentle heat and pressure to unblock the meibomian glands, which produce the oily outer layer of tears, for treating dry eyes. 
  • Punctual plugs: Another dry eye therapy, punctal plugs, are placed in the tear ducts to prevent tears from draining too quickly. 
  • Vision therapy: Personalized treatment plans are used to strengthen visual skills, eye coordination and alignment. 
  • Advanced retinal imaging: Provides early detection of macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma, among other conditions.

These in-office treatments can be combined with supplements at home for a comprehensive approach to eye care.

Tailoring Eye Care Solutions to Your Needs

Every one of our clients is different, with their own individual needs, which is why we offer personalized care plans. We take everything into account, including age and lifestyle factors, health history (e.g., family history of eye disease or diabetes), and specific eye conditions like dry eye or macular degeneration. 

With this information and knowledge, we can tailor our eye care solutions to your needs. You can also use at-home supplements for your visual health, benefiting from the best of both worlds for long-term eye health outcomes.

Prevention & Long-Term Eye Health Strategies

There are certain strategies that you can adopt to prevent early deterioration of your sight and maintain your eye health for the long term. These include: 

  • Consistent use of supplements for eye health in the long term
  • Regular eye exams to monitor for any developing issues
  • Making lifestyle changes such as limiting screen time and engaging in more outdoor activities to promote better vision
