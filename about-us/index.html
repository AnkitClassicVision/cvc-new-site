Our Story

A Family Practice,
Not a Corporate Chain

When Classic Vision Care first opened its doors in Kennesaw in 1998, we made a promise: to provide every patient with the kind of personalized, attentive care that only a family practice can offer.

Over 25 years later, that promise remains at the heart of everything we do. While big-box retailers and corporate chains have come and gone, we've stayed true to our roots. We know our patients by name. We remember their families. We take the time to answer every question.

Today, with two locations serving Kennesaw and East Cobb/Marietta, we combine that same personal touch with the latest technology in eye care. From comprehensive exams to our signature Dry Eye Spa treatments, we're here to protect your vision for generations to come.

"We don't just see patients. We build relationships that last a lifetime."

- Dr. Mital Patel, Lead Optometrist

Details That Matter

A more helpful guide to Meet Your Eye Care Family

Classic Vision Care is built around relationships. Patients come to us for thorough eye care, then stay because the experience feels personal. We listen, explain, and make sure you feel confident about your options.

We serve both adults and children, which helps families keep care consistent over time. With two convenient locations, it is easier to fit eye care into real life.

Quick ways to get more value from your visit

  • Bring your current glasses and contact lenses if you have them.
  • Share any symptoms or lifestyle needs so we can personalize recommendations.
  • Plan for dilation if recommended during the visit.
  • Ask questions. We take time to explain your options clearly.

Common questions

Clear answers help you move forward with confidence.

What can I expect from a visit for Meet Your Eye Care Family?

You can expect clear explanations and a plan that fits your life. We focus on listening first, then recommending options that make sense for your goals, budget, and comfort.

Do you have two locations?

Yes. We serve patients in Kennesaw and East Cobb/Marietta. Many families choose the location that is closest to home, work, or school.

Do you see children and adults?

Yes. We provide care for kids, teens, adults, and seniors. That makes it easier to keep vision care under one roof as your family grows.

What makes Classic Vision Care different?

Patients often tell us they appreciate the time we take. We pair modern diagnostic tools with a calm, personal approach so you leave with answers you understand.
